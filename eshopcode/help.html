How to buy a product

On purchase Items page you will find an “Purchase” button. When you click on this button your product will be added to your shopping list, and you will be redirected to your purchases list, where you can amend your product selection, by removing it from your list or by changing the quantity that you wish to buy. You will also be able to see what is currently in your list by looking at the purchases list.

After the product has been added to your list and you are happy with your selection, you can click on the “Bill” button. This action will redirect you the Secure Online eShopping web site.eShopping will allow you to complete your sale.

New Users: If you have shopped at any eShopping enabled store in the past, you will already have a eShopping account, and you would be able to login using your eShopping Account. If you are unsure if you have a eshopping account, you can register your by click on the “sign in new user” link, this will tell you if you are registered.

Non Registered Users: If you have not yet registered at any eshopping enabled store, you can complete the form provided. This will allow eShopping to know who you are and where to ship your products.

When your order is ready to be shipped you will receive another bill to notify you that your order is completed. At this stage the funds will no longer be reserved on your account, and will be moved from your account to our account.

If you need to cancel your order, you can do so by visiting to eshopping outlets, selecting the relevant order, and clicking on the “Remove Item” link at the bottom of your purchase list page. Just note that you can only cancel an order if the order has not been shipped yet.
